Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I reset my password?

Visit the login page and click "Forgot Password." Enter your registered email address and follow the instructions sent to your inbox.

What should I do if I experience technical issues during an exam?

Immediately contact emergency support at emergency@aiexamproctor.edu or call our emergency hotline. Take screenshots if possible to help us diagnose the issue.

How long does it take to get a response to my inquiry?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Emergency support for active exams is available 24/7.

Can I schedule a demo of the system?

Yes! Contact us to schedule a personalized demo for your institution. We'll walk you through all features and answer any questions.

Is there mobile app support?

Currently, we recommend using a desktop or laptop computer with a webcam for the best exam experience. Mobile support is planned for future updates.
