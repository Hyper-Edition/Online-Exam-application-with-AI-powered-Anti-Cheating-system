About AI Exam Proctoring System

Our Mission

To create a secure, reliable, and intelligent online examination environment that ensures academic integrity while providing a seamless experience for students, teachers, and administrators.

Project Overview

This innovative system leverages cutting-edge Artificial Intelligence technologies to detect and prevent cheating during online examinations. By combining multiple detection methods, we provide comprehensive monitoring that traditional proctoring systems cannot match.

The system uses face detection and recognition to verify student identity, object detection to identify prohibited items, and behavioral analysis to monitor suspicious activities during exams.
